Junior Research Data Scientist, March 2024 - April 2025

As a Junior Research Data Scientist in the credit risk team, I contributed to credit-risk forecasting for a retail credit portfolio. I participated in the development of an Early Warning System for delinquency detection, working with a hybrid ARIMA + XGBoost approach that combines behavioral time-series dynamics with structural borrower features. I also assisted in implementing online model-adaptation techniques that update risk signals between full retraining cycles as borrower behavior evolves. Additionally, I contributed to ML-based data-drift monitoring by applying classifier-based drift-detection methods to compare historical and incoming feature distributions. This helped the team detect shifts in portfolio-risk patterns earlier and adjust decision strategies before deterioration became visible in standard performance metrics.
